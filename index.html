Statutory Debt Resolution Solutions

香港法例提供多種債務解決途徑，讓債務人在合法框架下處理財務困難

Hong Kong legislation provides various debt resolution pathways for debtors to address financial difficulties within a legal framework

個人自願安排 (IVA)

Individual Voluntary Arrangement

根據《破產條例》第6章第20條，債務人可向法院申請臨時命令，在代名人監督下與債權人達成具法律約束力的還款協議。

Under Section 20 of the Bankruptcy Ordinance Cap. 6, debtors may apply to the Court for an interim order and reach a legally binding repayment agreement with creditors under nominee supervision.

債務紓緩計劃 (DRP)

Debt Relief Plan

無需經法庭程序，透過專業顧問直接與個別債權人協商，達成較靈活的還款安排，適用於債務規模較小或債權人數目有限的情況。

Without court proceedings, negotiate directly with individual creditors through professional consultants to reach flexible repayment arrangements, suitable for smaller debts or limited number of creditors.

結餘轉戶貸款

Debt Consolidation Loan

將多項高息債務整合為單一低息貸款，簡化還款安排並降低整體利息支出，適用於信貸評級尚可的債務人。

Consolidate multiple high-interest debts into a single low-interest loan, simplifying repayment arrangements and reducing overall interest expenses, suitable for debtors with acceptable credit ratings.

重要法律資訊

Important Legal Information

根據《破產條例》(第6章)，破產令一經頒布，破產人將受到多項限制，包括但不限於：不得擔任公司董事、從事受規管行業工作可能受影響、資產可被變賣還債、出入境可能受限制等。個人自願安排(IVA)提供一個法律認可的替代方案，讓債務人在避免上述後果的情況下，與債權人達成還款協議。

Under the Bankruptcy Ordinance (Cap. 6), once a bankruptcy order is made, the bankrupt will be subject to various restrictions, including but not limited to: prohibition from acting as a company director, potential impact on employment in regulated industries, assets may be sold to repay debts, and travel restrictions may apply. Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) provides a legally recognized alternative, allowing debtors to reach repayment agreements with creditors while avoiding the aforementioned consequences.

常見問題

FAQ

債務重組常見疑問

Frequently Asked Questions

當債務人面臨以下情況時，IVA可能是合適的選擇：(1) 無力按現有條款償還債務；(2) 有穩定收入來源；(3) 希望避免破產帶來的職業限制及社會影響；(4) 債務金額較大或債權人數目較多。IVA讓債務人在法律框架下與債權人達成還款協議，通常可削減30-70%的債務本金。

IVA may be suitable when: (1) Unable to repay debts under current terms; (2) Have stable income source; (3) Wish to avoid career restrictions and social impact of bankruptcy; (4) Larger debt amount or multiple creditors. IVA allows debtors to reach repayment agreements with creditors within a legal framework, typically reducing 30-70% of the principal debt.

主要分別包括：(1) 破產會留下公開紀錄8年，而IVA完成後不會有破產紀錄；(2) 破產期間不得擔任公司董事或從事某些專業，IVA則無此限制；(3) 破產人的資產可能被變賣還債，IVA通常可保留主要資產；(4) 破產需經過4年的破產期，而IVA還款期一般為4-6年但較為靈活。

Key differences include: (1) Bankruptcy leaves a public record for 8 years, while IVA completion leaves no bankruptcy record; (2) During bankruptcy, one cannot serve as company director or practice certain professions, IVA has no such restrictions; (3) Bankrupt's assets may be sold to repay debts, IVA usually preserves major assets; (4) Bankruptcy requires a 4-year period, while IVA repayment is typically 4-6 years but more flexible.

申請IVA的基本條件包括：(1) 必須有穩定的收入來源；(2) 無力按現有條款償還債務；(3) 需獲得持有75%或以上債務金額的債權人同意；(4) 需委任合資格的代名人處理申請。申請人需向法庭提交建議書，說明還款計劃及理由，經債權人會議投票通過後，法庭會頒布命令使計劃具法律約束力。

Basic requirements for IVA include: (1) Must have stable income source; (2) Unable to repay debts under current terms; (3) Requires approval from creditors holding 75% or more of the debt; (4) Must appoint a qualified nominee to handle the application. Applicants must submit a proposal to the court explaining the repayment plan, which becomes legally binding after creditor meeting vote approval and court order.

選擇取決於個別情況：(1) DRP適合債權人數目較少（通常1-3個）的情況，可較快完成；(2) IVA適合債權人較多、債務金額較大的情況，提供法律保障；(3) DRP無需法庭程序，費用較低但不具法律約束力；(4) IVA經法庭認可，一旦通過所有債權人均受約束。建議先進行評估，由專業顧問根據您的情況提供建議。

The choice depends on individual circumstances: (1) DRP suits cases with fewer creditors (usually 1-3), faster completion; (2) IVA suits cases with more creditors and larger debts, provides legal protection; (3) DRP requires no court proceedings, lower cost but not legally binding; (4) IVA is court-approved, once passed all creditors are bound. Recommend assessment first, with professional consultants providing advice based on your situation.
