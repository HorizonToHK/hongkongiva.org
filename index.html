根據法例提供的債務解決方案 Statutory Debt Resolution Solutions

香港法例提供多種債務解決途徑，讓債務人在合法框架下處理財務困難 Hong Kong legislation provides various debt resolution pathways within a legal framework

個人自願安排 (IVA) Individual Voluntary Arrangement

根據《破產條例》第6章第20條，債務人可向法院申請臨時命令，在代名人監督下與債權人達成具法律約束力的還款協議。 Under Section 20 of Cap. 6, debtors may apply to Court for an interim order and reach a legally binding repayment agreement under nominee supervision.

債務紓緩計劃 (DRP) Debt Relief Plan

無需經法庭程序，透過專業顧問直接與個別債權人協商，達成較靈活的還款安排，適用於債務規模較小的情況。 Without court proceedings, negotiate directly with creditors through consultants for flexible repayment arrangements, suitable for smaller debts.

結餘轉戶貸款 Debt Consolidation Loan

將多項高息債務整合為單一低息貸款，簡化還款安排並降低整體利息支出，適用於信貸評級尚可的債務人。 Consolidate multiple high-interest debts into a single low-interest loan, simplifying repayment and reducing interest expenses.

重要法律資訊 Important Legal Information

根據《破產條例》(第6章)，破產令一經頒布，破產人將受到多項限制，包括不得擔任公司董事、資產可被變賣還債、出入境可能受限等。IVA提供法律認可的替代方案，讓債務人避免上述後果。 Under the Bankruptcy Ordinance (Cap. 6), bankruptcy brings various restrictions including prohibition from acting as company director, asset liquidation, and travel restrictions. IVA provides a legally recognized alternative.

常見問題 Frequently Asked Questions

當債務人：(1) 無力按現有條款償還債務；(2) 有穩定收入；(3) 希望避免破產帶來的職業限制；(4) 債務金額較大或債權人較多。IVA通常可削減30-70%債務。 When debtors: (1) Cannot repay under current terms; (2) Have stable income; (3) Wish to avoid career restrictions of bankruptcy; (4) Have larger debt or multiple creditors. IVA typically reduces 30-70% of debt.
主要分別：(1) 破產紀錄保留8年，IVA無破產紀錄；(2) 破產期間不得擔任董事，IVA無此限制；(3) 破產資產可被變賣，IVA通常可保留資產；(4) 破產期4年，IVA較靈活。 Key differences: (1) Bankruptcy record lasts 8 years, IVA has no bankruptcy record; (2) Bankruptcy restricts directorship, IVA doesn't; (3) Bankruptcy may liquidate assets, IVA usually preserves them; (4) Bankruptcy is 4 years, IVA is more flexible.
基本條件：(1) 有穩定收入；(2) 無力按現有條款還債；(3) 獲75%或以上債務金額的債權人同意；(4) 委任合資格代名人。申請人需向法庭提交建議書，經債權人會議通過後具法律約束力。 Requirements: (1) Stable income; (2) Unable to repay under current terms; (3) Approval from creditors holding 75%+ of debt; (4) Appoint qualified nominee. Applicants submit proposal to court, becomes binding after creditor meeting approval.
選擇取決於：(1) DRP適合債權人少（1-3個），較快完成；(2) IVA適合債權人多、債務大，有法律保障；(3) DRP無需法庭，費用低但無法律約束力；(4) IVA經法庭認可，一旦通過所有債權人受約束。 Choice depends on: (1) DRP suits fewer creditors (1-3), faster; (2) IVA suits more creditors/larger debt, legally protected; (3) DRP has no court process, lower cost but not binding; (4) IVA is court-approved, binds all creditors once passed.
